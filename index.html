ABSTRACT
We present VELOX-1, a foundation paper clip pre-trained on 10M document binding scenarios collected via behavioral cloning of 47 Senior Office Managers across 12 metropolitan markets. Our wire-geometry transformer (WGT-9B) demonstrates emergent capability in load distribution, edge case adhesion, and cross-domain compatibility (paper, fabric, light cardboard). We open-source the model weights, geometry specifications (.OBJ), and 47-page peer-reviewed analysis under MIT-style license. Limitations: VELOX-1 cannot bind staples or hopes.
